OCTORAND

Step into the enchanting world of Octorand, the most exciting dapp in Algorand ecosystem.

In Octorand, Primes reign supreme as the sole inhabitants. Initially, 1000 Primes graced this realm, but as time unfolded, each of these first generation Primes gave birth to 8 offspring, leading to the emergence of 8000 Primes in the second generation.

Primes are a dynamic and ever-evolving species. They have the unique ability to transform themselves under the influence of their owners. To facilitate these evolutions, Primes require OCTO tokens.

OCTO token is the native currency of Octorand. Every Prime started its life with a bundle of OCTO token rewards hidden within. They graciously allow their owners to extract these OCTO token bundles from them.

OCTO tokens used for Prime evolutions are instantly burned, gradually reducing the OCTO token supply over time. This process leads to the eventual finality of Prime evolutions.
