OCTORAND

Step into the enchanting world of Octorand, the most exciting dapp in Algorand ecosystem.

In Octorand, Primes reign supreme as the sole inhabitants. Initially, 1000 Primes graced this realm, but as time unfolded, each of these first generation Primes gave birth to 8 offspring, leading to the emergence of 8000 Primes in the second generation.

Primes are a dynamic and ever-evolving species. They have the unique ability to transform themselves based on the influence of their owners. To facilitate these evolutions, Primes require OCTO tokens.
