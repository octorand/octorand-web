Guide

Octorand is a unique virtual world exclusively inhabited by beings known as Primes. The characteristics and abilities of Primes are governed by OCTO, the native currency of this digital realm.

Primes

Each Prime in Octorand is an Algorand NFT, uniquely represented by an Algorand ASA and an Algorand Smart Contract. Primes exist in two generations, the first generation consists of 1000 Primes, each of which produced 8 offspring, resulting in 8000 second generation Primes. The two generations of Primes differ in several ways, with the first generation typically being more costly to manage compared to the second generation.

Every Prime begins its journey with an OCTO rewards bundle. First generation Primes come loaded with 1000 OCTO, while second generation Primes contain 8 OCTO each. Prime owners can withdraw these OCTO rewards at their convenience.

Tokenomics

OCTO, represented by an Algorand ASA, is the native currency of Octorand and features unique deflationary dynamics. As a versatile utility token, OCTO serves numerous applications across the Octorand ecosystem. With an initial finite supply of 1,000,000 tokens, the total amount of OCTO gradually decreases over time as it is utilized for various functions within Octorand.

Transforming

Primes are a dynamic and ever-evolving species. They have the unique ability to transform themselves under the influence of their owners. To facilitate these evolutions, Primes require OCTO tokens.

One of the key features of Primes is the ability to rename them. First generation Primes have 8-letter names, while second generation Primes have 16-letter names. Initially, Primes are given random names, but owners can rename their Primes to any name they choose, letter by letter.

The cost of renaming a letter depends on the alphabetical distance between the current letter and the target letter. For example, changing 'A' to 'B' is a one-letter change, while changing 'B' to 'D' is a two-letter change and costs twice as much. Changing 'Z' to 'X' is a three-letter change and costs three times as much. The OCTO tokens used for renaming are burned instantly.

Prime owners can personalize the appearance of their Primes by selecting from 3 unique skins and 16 vibrant color themes. The OCTO tokens used for repainting Primes are burned instantly, decreasing the total OCTO supply.
