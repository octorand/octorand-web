Guide

Octorand is a virtual world where the sole inhabitants are called Primes. Features of Primes are controlled by OCTO, the native currency of Octorand.

Primes

Each Prime is a Algorand NFT. They are uniquely represented by a Algorand ASA and a Algorand Smart Contract. Primes come in two generations. There are 1000 first generation Primes who each gave birth to 8 offspring leading to 8000 Primes in the second generation.
