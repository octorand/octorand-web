Tokenomics

OCTO token, the native currency of Octorand platform comes with interesting deflationary dynamics. It is a multi purpose utility token with many use cases across the entire Octorand ecosystem.

SUMMARY

Each Prime started with a OCTO token rewards bundle burried inside them. Full supply of OCTO token was distributed among the Prime holders in this manner.
