Traits

Primes earn badges by meeting specific qualifications. Some badges, like Artifact, are inherent from birth. Others, like Chameleon, are earned as Primes undergo transformations. Additionally, badges such as Wordsmith are awarded when Primes change their names.

SUMMARY

Each Prime started with a OCTO token rewards bundle burried inside them. Full supply of OCTO token was distributed among the Prime holders in this manner.

SUPPLY

The OCTO supply is finite and hyper-deflationary. Supply is burned each time users do things like transform their Primes, upgrade Prime artwork, etc.
